# Rememberry — Setup Guide

## Files in this folder
- index.html   ← the app
- manifest.json ← makes it installable as a PWA
- sw.js         ← lets it work offline
- icons/        ← app icon (home screen)

---

## How to get it on your phone

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub repository
1. Go to https://github.com and sign in
2. Click the **+** icon (top right) → **New repository**
3. Name it exactly: `rememberry`
4. Set it to **Public**
5. Do NOT tick "Add a README"
6. Click **Create repository**

### Step 2 — Upload the files
1. On the new repo page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag and drop ALL files and the icons folder:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icons/icon-192.png
   - icons/icon-512.png
3. Scroll down, click **Commit changes**

### Step 3 — Turn on GitHub Pages
1. Go to your repo → **Settings** tab
2. Scroll to **Pages** in the left menu
3. Under "Branch", select **main** and click **Save**
4. Wait 1-2 minutes, then your app is live at:
   https://YOUR-GITHUB-USERNAME.github.io/rememberry/

### Step 4 — Install on your S24 Ultra
1. Open **Chrome** on your phone
2. Go to your URL above
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (top right)
4. Tap **Add to Home screen**
5. Tap **Add**

Rememberry now lives on your home screen like any other app.
Your tasks are saved locally on your phone and work offline.
